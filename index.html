❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export my audio?

Tap the Export button in your project, choose your preferred format (MP3, WAV, or M4A) and quality settings, then tap Export. You can save to Files or share directly.

How do AI coins work?

AI coins are used to access premium AI features like music generation, text-to-speech, voice cloning, and sound effects. Purchase coin packages through the in-app store. Different AI features use different amounts of coins based on complexity.

Can I restore my purchases?

Yes! Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. This will restore any coin packages or premium features you've purchased, even on a new device.

How many tracks can I add?

VoxBox supports unlimited audio tracks in your projects. You can mix and edit as many tracks as you need for your production.

What AI features are available?

VoxBox offers AI music generation, text-to-speech with 120+ voices, sound effects generation, speech-to-text transcription, and vocal isolation (stem separation).

Where are my projects saved?

Projects are automatically saved to your device. You can also backup projects to cloud storage through the Export/Share feature.

Is there a free version?

Yes! VoxBox is free to download with full multitrack editing capabilities. AI features require coins, which can be purchased in packages starting at $2.99.
