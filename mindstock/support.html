Subscriptions (MindStock Pro)

MindStock Pro unlocks all AI tools, up to 10 portfolios, the AI engine chooser and Yahoo import. It's offered as a monthly or yearly auto-renewing subscription.

  • Manage or cancel: on iOS, open Settings → your name → Subscriptions. On Android, open Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.
  • Restore purchases: sign in with the same account and your premium will reappear automatically (it is tied to your account across both platforms).
  • Billing issues: refunds are handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store) per their policies.

Delete your account

In the app, open Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, portfolios and data. You can also email us to request deletion.
